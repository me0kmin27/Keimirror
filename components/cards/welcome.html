Welcome to Keimirror

Keimirror mirrors Linux distributions and open-source repositories from Keiverse's own infrastructure in Gumi, South Korea. It focuses on fast domestic access, official mirror participation, CDN-assisted resilience, and predictable synchronization from real-time updates to daily refreshes.

Gumi-based mirror Runs from Keiverse's private IDC environment in Gumi for Korean and nearby networks.
High-speed access Uses dual 1 Gbps dedicated ISP uplinks with load balancing for stable throughput.
Official mirror coverage Serves official mirrors including Arch Linux Tier 1, Gentoo, Kali, CachyOS, Artix, and OPNsense.
Regular synchronization Repository jobs refresh from real time to 24-hour intervals depending on upstream needs.
