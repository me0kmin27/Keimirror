Welcome to Keimirror

Keimirror is the public mirror service of Keiverse. Keiverse has provided privacy-focused alternative services since 2023 and operates its infrastructure from a private data center in Gumi, South Korea. The mirror is designed for low-latency access to Linux distributions and open-source packages for users in Korea and nearby networks.

South Korea infrastructure Operated from Keiverse's own data-center environment in Gumi.
Dual-ISP network Designed around two 1 Gbps dedicated uplinks with load balancing.
Privacy-first services Part of a broader Keiverse ecosystem of privacy-focused alternatives.
Synchronized operations Keiverse services are coordinated with the project's own time-server setup.

Disclaimer

When an upstream project lists Keimirror as an official mirror, this service is an official distribution endpoint for that project. Keimirror still serves mirrored files as provided by upstream projects and does not change their licenses, release policies, package contents, or security advisories.

The service is operated on a best-effort basis. Synchronization delays, maintenance windows, routing changes, or abusive traffic controls may temporarily affect availability. Always verify critical downloads with the checksums and signatures published by the upstream project.
